Static Base

This is a simple boiler plate for static websites. It includes:

  • Support for Coffeescript & LESS
  • Integrates Grunt w/ Jekyll
  • Live Reload support for quick editing
  • A simple build process for producing a production site.
  • An initial structure for your includes, styles and javascript.
  • ...And lots more.
